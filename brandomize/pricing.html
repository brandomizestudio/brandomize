Pricing & Service Questions for Hisar Businesses

Get answers to common questions about our services and pricing in Hisar.

Are these prices fixed or customizable?
▼

Our prices are flexible based on your specific needs. The listed prices are starting points. For complex projects or custom requirements, we provide tailored quotes. Contact us for a personalized estimate for your Hisar business.

Do you offer discounts for bundled services?
▼

Yes! We offer package deals when you combine multiple services. For example, website + social media management, or brand identity + reel creation packages get 10-15% discount. Our Complete Business Bundle offers the best value for Hisar startups looking for comprehensive marketing solutions.

What's included in your website development packages?
▼

All our website packages include responsive design, mobile optimization, SEO basics, SSL certificate, hosting setup assistance, and 30 days of post-launch support. Business and e-commerce sites also get animations, contact forms, and Google Analytics integration.

What's your payment structure?
▼

For one-time projects (logos, websites, apps): 50% advance, 50% on delivery. For monthly services (social media management): Monthly billing with first month in advance. We accept bank transfers, UPI, and online payments. All prices are in INR and inclusive of taxes.

How do I choose the right package for my business?
▼

Start with your goals and budget. For new businesses in Hisar, we recommend starting with brand identity + basic website. For growing businesses, social media management + reels work great. Need help deciding? Book a free consultation and we'll recommend the best package for your needs.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?
▼

Yes! All projects come with 30 days of free support. After that, we offer maintenance packages starting at ₹2,000/month for websites, or hourly support at ₹500/hour. Monthly retainer clients get priority support and discounted rates on additional services.
